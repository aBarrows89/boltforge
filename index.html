REFUND
POLICY

Last updated: March 26, 2026

This Refund Policy applies to all custom software development, IT support, and related services provided by BoltForge ("we," "us," or "our"). By engaging our services, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Completed Software — No Refunds

Custom software that has been built, delivered, and deployed is not eligible for a refund. Because every BoltForge project is custom-built to the client's specifications, completed deliverables represent labor, expertise, and resources that cannot be recovered or resold.

This applies to:

  • Fully delivered projects that meet the specifications outlined in the signed statement of work (SOW)
  • Projects that have been deployed to production, regardless of whether the client has begun using the platform
  • Source code that has been transferred to the client

2. Cancellation During Development — Prorated Refund

If a project is cancelled by the client before completion, BoltForge will issue a prorated refund calculated as follows:

What the client pays for:

  • Labor at standard rates: All hours worked by BoltForge developers, designers, and engineers up to the date of cancellation, billed at our standard labor rate of $125 per hour
  • Services rendered: Any third-party services procured on behalf of the client (e.g., domain registration, cloud infrastructure provisioning, software licenses, API subscriptions) that have already been paid for or cannot be reversed
  • Milestone deliverables: Any completed project milestones as defined in the SOW, billed at the milestone rate agreed upon in the contract

Refund calculation:

The refund amount equals the total amount paid to date, minus the sum of labor costs (hours worked x $125/hr), third-party fees already incurred, and any completed milestone charges. If the cost of work performed exceeds the amount paid, the client will be invoiced for the remaining balance.

Cancellation process:

  • Cancellation must be submitted in writing via email to hello@boltforge.io
  • BoltForge will provide an itemized breakdown of all work completed and fees incurred within 10 business days of the cancellation request
  • Refunds will be processed within 30 business days of the finalized cancellation statement
  • All work product completed up to the cancellation date remains the property of the client, subject to full payment of amounts owed

3. Monthly Hosting & Support

Monthly hosting and support fees are billed in advance and are non-refundable for the current billing period. Clients may cancel hosting and support with 30 days' written notice. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

  • No partial-month refunds will be issued
  • Upon cancellation, BoltForge will provide a full code and data export within 14 business days
  • The client is responsible for migrating to their own hosting after the cancellation effective date

4. IT Support Services

IT support services billed on a per-incident or hourly basis are non-refundable once the service has been rendered. For monthly managed IT plans, the same cancellation terms as hosting apply (30 days' notice, non-refundable for the current period).

5. Disputes

If you believe a charge is incorrect or wish to dispute a refund calculation, contact us at hello@boltforge.io within 30 days of receiving the itemized statement. We will review the dispute in good faith and respond within 10 business days.
